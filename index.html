Research

For my Ph.D.research work, I am interested in exploring trajectory optimization and safe-control methods for spacecraft and aircraft systems. My main goal involves developing an optimization framework for path reconfiguration when faults or unexpected situations occur, that threatens the mission goals. For this end, I am currently learning about convex optimization and exploring space visualization software such as the NAIF toolkit: Small-Body Identification Tool (SBDT) from NASA JPL, among others.
